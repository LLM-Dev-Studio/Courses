This module helps your team assess capability, data readiness, process fit, and change readiness before implementation.

> [!NOTE]
>
> **What you'll learn:**
> - What this topic means in daily business operations
> - How to apply it in a practical, low-risk way
> - What controls and ownership checks are needed before scaling

## Why This Matters for Your Business

This module helps your team assess capability, data readiness, process fit, and change readiness before implementation.

In practice, this topic supports safer delivery, more consistent service quality, and better use of team time when paired with clear accountability.

## Practical Application Steps

1. Define one clear workflow where this topic can improve speed or consistency.
2. Set a quality bar for what "good" looks like before rolling out.
3. Assign an owner for human review and final sign-off.
4. Measure outcomes (time saved, quality improved, errors reduced).
5. Scale only after controls, documentation, and team confidence are in place.

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

- Treating AI output as final instead of a first draft
- Skipping review for customer, legal, or safety-related content
- Trying to scale too quickly without a measurable pilot
- Failing to document ownership and escalation pathways

> [!TIP]
>
> Keep implementation simple: one use case, one owner, one success metric.
