## How It Works

The course has 8 modules with short lessons and quizzes. You can complete it in order or focus on the areas most relevant to your role first.

## The JJ's Waste & Recycling Context

This course is grounded in JJ's operating environment:
- Australian operations across domestic, commercial, and industrial services
- Service lines including general waste, recycling, organics, liquid and hazardous streams, and related operational services
- Council and community service obligations
- Large-scale council servicing and high-volume weekly collection operations
- Safety, compliance, and customer service priorities
- Practical examples relevant to waste and recycling workflows
