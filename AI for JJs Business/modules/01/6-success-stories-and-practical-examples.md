# Success Stories and Practical Examples

Examples of practical AI assistance in waste and recycling contexts:
- Drafting customer follow-up communications from service notes
- Converting long incident notes into concise internal summaries
- Creating first-draft toolbox talk outlines from recurring issues
- Producing structured weekly reporting narratives from raw bullet points
- Preparing route exception summaries for depot handover
- Drafting service explanations for domestic, commercial, and industrial customer enquiries
- Creating first-pass summaries for recycling contamination trends and recurring service issues

In each case, teams still validate content before use.
