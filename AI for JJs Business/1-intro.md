# Welcome to AI for JJ's Waste & Recycling

You have probably heard a lot about AI lately. It can sound technical, expensive, or hard to apply in field operations. The good news is that practical AI can help many JJ's teams right now, without changing what already works.

This course is designed for real work in an Australian waste and recycling business: depot teams, drivers, operations coordinators, customer service, workshop and maintenance teams, account managers, and leaders.

JJ's context matters here. JJ's Waste & Recycling is a long-established, wholly Australian owned and operated business with nationwide service delivery, more than 2,000 employees, and broad service coverage across domestic, commercial, industrial, and specialist waste streams.

## What You'll Learn

By the end of this course, you'll be able to:
- **Understand** what AI actually is (and isn't) — without the jargon
- **Assess** whether your team is ready for AI and where to start
- **Choose** the right AI tools for your specific needs and budget
- **Use AI** for everyday tasks like marketing, admin, communication, planning, and reporting support
- **Stay legal** with AU privacy law, the Fair Trading Act, and your responsibilities
- **Implement AI** step-by-step without wasting money on the wrong things
- **Set up** simple governance so your team uses AI safely
- **Plan ahead** for where AI is going and what it means for your business

## Who This Is For

This course is for JJ's staff and managers who want practical outcomes, including:
- Operations and depot teams
- Customer service and account teams
- Fleet, workshop, and maintenance teams
- IT teams
- Supervisors and business leaders

**No technical skills required**. If you can use email and a smartphone, you can do this course.
